Chicken Mandi Recipe

Time: 2 hours | Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 whole chicken (cut into halves)
  • 2 tablespoons mandi spice mix
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 3 cups chicken broth
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Marinate the chicken with mandi spice mix, salt, and black pepper. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat olive oil and saut√© onions and garlic until golden brown.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, cinnamon stick, and cook until tomatoes soften.
  4. Place the marinated chicken on top and pour in the chicken broth.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 45 minutes, until the chicken is tender.
  6. Remove the chicken and grill or broil until golden brown.
  7. Meanwhile, add washed basmati rice to the broth and cook until fully done.
  8. Serve the rice with the grilled chicken on top.

What to Serve With Chicken Mandi

This dish pairs well with:

  • Yogurt-based sauces
  • Fresh salad
  • Pickles

Storage Tips

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat on the stove or microwave before serving.
