Pesto Pasta Recipe

Time: 15 minutes | Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 ½ tablespoons pesto
  • Salt to taste
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Boil salted water in a large pot and cook the pasta for 8-10 minutes until al dente. Drain and transfer to a bowl.
  2. Heat olive oil in a frying pan over medium-low heat. Add chopped onion and cook for 3 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in pesto, salt, and black pepper, and cook until warmed through.
  4. Mix the pesto mixture with the hot pasta, add grated Parmesan, and toss well to coat.

What to Serve With Pesto Pasta

This versatile dish pairs well with various proteins and sides:

  • Chicken, sausage, or chickpeas
  • French Baguettes
  • Classic Restaurant Caesar Salad
  • Balsamic Bruschetta

Storage Tips

Store leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 3-5 days. Reheat in the microwave or on the stove.
